Projects

I have had the ability to work on different projects ranging from being a product designer to a UX/UI designer on mobile and web applications. I approach my work with the question "How may I improve my user's need and increasing my stakeholders KPIs?" In order to answer and meet my goal, I follow the UX Double Diamond methodology. I start by developing an understanding and empathy for my user, define my observations and problem, ideate potential solutions, create prototypes and test with several iterations between each stage. Below are a few of my projects. Please look around and contact me to discuss problems, solutions and any feedback. I am always looking to improve my next design.
